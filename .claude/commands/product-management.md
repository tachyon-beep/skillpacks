# Using Product Management

<!-- Content authority lives in plugins/axiom-product-management/skills/using-product-management/SKILL.md. Do not add inline router content to this command. -->

## Overview

Product management here means standing autonomous ownership of a software product: holding the bet, preserving continuity, and validating whether value landed. It is stateful ownership, not a one-off PRD, backlog grooming pass, or feature request.

## When to Use

Use this wrapper when a Claude is being asked to own a product across sessions, decide what is worth building and why, turn opportunities into falsifiable specs, validate shipped value, or checkpoint durable product state.

Do not use this for building one feature, choosing an architecture, or running the delivery flow of already-decided work.
